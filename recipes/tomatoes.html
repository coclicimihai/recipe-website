Ricotta And Herb Stuffed Tomatoes

Info

A lovely and refreshing dish to enjoy in summer or anytime you want

Ingredients

  • 3 plum tomatoes
  • 150g ricotta cheese
  • 30g of mixed herbs(parsley, dill, chives)
  • 1 garlic clove minced
  • 10 ml olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Method

Mix the ricotta with the chopped herbs, garlic, salt, pepper and set aside. Wash the tomatoes and cut them in half, then scoop the seeds and pulp leaving the shell intact. Place the ricotta mix into a piping bag and stuff the tomatoes with the mix.

To serve, place the tomatoes on the plate and garnish with more chives and drizzle some olive oil on the top. Enjoy.
