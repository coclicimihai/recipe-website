Mediterranean Quinoa And Chickpeas Croquettes

Info

A lovely and easy to make vegan dish for your vegan friends or just something more healthy that you can enjoy with your friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 150g> quinoa
  • 200g cooked chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 30g sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 30g pitted Kalamata olives, chopped
  • 19g chopped parsley
  • 10g chopped mint
  • 2 garlic cloves, grated
  • 2 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 200g breadcrumbs

Method

In a pot add the quinoa and water to cover the quinoa with 1 or 2cm and cook it until soft and there is no water left, (be careful not to become to watery or soggy) if it is cooked and you think it is a bit to wet just cover the pot with cling film and leave it for few minutes. I will recommend to buy the chickpeas already cooke as a tin because it will make your life way more easy than cooking yourself the chickpeas but if you love that then soak the chickpeas over night in water and next day put them to boil until soft, if you got the tin chickpeas reserve the water (as if you cooked your own chickpeas reserve some of the water), drain the chickpeas and rinse them and after place them in a blender and crush them, don't turn them into a paste, you want bits inside your croquettes. Prepare the rest of the ingredients and place everything into a bowl and mix,but use just half of the breadcrumbs. After take the mix and place it into a KitchenAid and using the pallet start mixing and while you mix add some of the reserved chickpeas water until becomes a bit wet and starts to stick so you can roll the mix into shape. Now that the mix is ready you need to shape the croquettes, i will recommend to make them around 30 to 35g as it will be easy to cook and will be a 2 bite food, then roll it trough the rest of the breadcrumb that you got left and deep fry them at 170C until nice and golden brown.

Lemon and Tahini Dressing

Ingredients

  • 50g tahini paste
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • #
  • 3 tablespoons of water
  • salt and pepper to taste

Method

Add all ingredients into a bowl aside of water and whisk them together and add the water slowly while whisking until becomes smooth and creamy.

To serve, fry the croquettes and place them on the plate and then drizzle some of the tahini dressing on the top and garnish with micro coriander. Enjoy.
