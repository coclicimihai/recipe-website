Crispy Camembert with tropical mango salsa

Info

Camembert is a moist, soft and creamy cheese made of cow milk that was first made in the late 18th century in Camembert, Normandy, northwest of France. it is compared in look and taste to Brie cheese but has a slightly lower butterfat content than Brie, around 20 to 25% by weight.

Ingredients

  • 1 Camembert cheese wheen(2 portions)
  • 3 eggs
  • 150g breadcrumbs
  • 80g white flour
  • 10g salt
  • 10g black cracked pepper

Method

Cut the Camembert cheese wheel into wedges, then in a bowl prepare the flour and season it with salt and pepper, in another bowl add the eggs and whisk them. Take the Camembert wedge and pass it trough the flour and remove any excess of flour, then pass it trough the egg and after trough the breadcrumb, repeat to have a nice coating and to prevent any leaking of cheese. Heat the oil at 170C and fry them until golden brown.

Tropical Mango Salsa

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and diced(small)
  • 50g red bell pepper diced(small)
  • 25g red onion finely chopped
  • 15g fresh coriander chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method

After you prepped everything add them in a bowl, squeeze the lime juice over the mix and toss gently to combine then season with salt and pepper to taste.

To serve, place 3 Camembert wedges on a plate and then the salsa around. Plating is all up to you, be creative and impress everyone. Enjoy.
